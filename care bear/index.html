About Us

At Care Bear Day Care & Learning Center, your child is our priority.

With our qualified educators and friendly staff, you can rest assured that your child is in safe and loving hands.

We believe that children not only need education to be successful, but also love and a nurturing hand to guide them and help them grow into the best version of themselves.

As working mums and dads we are always cautious about the kind of people our children are surrounded by when we are not around, which is the reason why Care Bear Day Care & Learning Center was started, to provide working mums and dads peace of mind that your children are safe and well taken care of.
